Example 5 - ItemsControllers

This is the Example5Controller.

The Example5Controller has an ItemsController that is a special kind of BindingController.

The ItemsController combines RivetsJS' capability to iterate creating children controllers when having a list as model with flow's capability of having composable controllers.
